  1. Hack Your High School

    Get a group of youth together to remix their schools’ web site. With our Xray Goggles tool, it's as easy as going to a school's website and changing some text to announce Summer's coming early or adding an image making Justin Bieber the new school mascot.

    YouTube Karaoke

    Participants will pick their favorite song from YouTube, lip-sync their own version on camera, and mash the two together to create their very own music video. Who knows? Maybe you'll discover the next YouTube sensation.

    Private Eye

    Participants will create their own Wizard of Oz moment by pulling back the curtain to see who's watching us on the Web. Turn on our Lightbeam tool, visit a handful of websites, and reveal who's tracking you. You'll never look at the Web the same way again.
